Speaker Details

Jugal Porwal

Jugal Porwal is a Computer Engineering Graduate from University of Pune. In his 6+ years of professional career, he has been associated with VMWare, EllieMae, and Diebold Nixdorf. During employment, he has worked at different positions such as Build and Release Engineer, System Engineer and Devops Engineer. He is currently working as a Senior Cloud Engineer at Diebold Nixdorf. He has special interest in the research areas of cloud computing specifically around CI/CD, Infrastructure Automation and App Virtualization
