Speaker Details

The Impact of IEEE Standards on Internet of Things

Srikanth Chandrasekaran

Sri has been associated with the IEEE Standards Association (IEEE SA) and the IEEE India office for the past 8 years as a Sr Director, with a global focus on IoT and Foundational Technologies practice. In this role, Sri is focused on identifying and growing standardization programs in IoT/Smart Cities, Sensors, Blockchain and AR/VR. Sri also heads the standardization activities for IEEE SA in India and for the larger Asia Pacific region. As part of his overall portfolio, Sri manages the IEEE Blended Learning Program (BLP), an IEEE education platform, focused on training and skills development for students as well as industry professionals.

Prior to joining IEEE, Sri was associated with Freescale Semiconductor Inc. (formerly Motorola Inc.) for 18 years, managing a global Electronic Design Automation R&D team focused on modeling of mixed signal designs and Electro-magnetic compliance for Freescale products.

Sri received the Accellera Technical Excellence Award in 2009 for his leadership and contributions to design automation standardisation activities. Sri holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Physics from Madras University, India and a Postgraduate degree in Electrical Communication from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India.
